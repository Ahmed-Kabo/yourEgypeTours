YOUR SAFETY AND SECURITY

Your well-being is of the foremost importance to us, and we’ll do everything we can to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Our emergency phone line has a member of our staff available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. All leaders, guides and/or drivers are fully licensed, trained, experienced, and adhere to strict guidelines. In addition, you will never do anything you do not feel comfortable doing and we will always make sure that we do not take you to places where you will feel unsafe.

TAILOR MADE HOLIDAYS

Tailor made holidays are exactly what you’d hope they would be: bespoke, unique and personal to you. Choose the date you depart, how long you stay and activities that fit you. RESPONSIBLE TOURISM AND ECOTOURISM

Your Egypt Tours is committed to sustainable travel and minimizing its impact on the environment and to reducing its dependence on non-renewable resources. We are committed to complying with all relevant legislation and/or regulations to ensure sustainable tourism. Your Egypt Tours maintains a policy of “Leave Only Footprints and Take Only Photos.” Our staff and major service providers strive to ensure that no removal of any natural or cultural artifacts or materials to ensure our environment looks and functions the same before and after our visit.
